Paper Cup Holder Technical Specifications

Material Composition

  • 100% food-grade kraft paper or white cardboard
  • Water-based, non-toxic adhesives
  • Optional PE coating for liquid resistance
  • Recyclable paper materials

Temperature Tolerance

  • Heat resistant: Up to 90°C (194°F) for standard versions
  • Up to 110°C (230°F) for PE-coated versions
  • Not microwave-safe unless specified
  • Freezer-safe: Down to -15°C (5°F)

Environmental Properties

  • Made from sustainably sourced paper fibers

Physical Characteristics

  • Available in 2-cup, 4-cup, and 6-cup configurations
  • Weight capacity: 1.5kg per cup slot
  • Finger hole designs for easy carrying
  • Stackable when empty for storage
  • Custom printing options available
